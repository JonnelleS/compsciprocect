This is Math 101


To learn math first you must understand the key elements:

  • Addition "+"
  • Subtraction "-"
  • Multiplication "*"
  • Division "รท"

Adding is when numbers are collectively added to the other for example 1+1=2 or 3+4=7 which is learned by counting on fingers. Subtraction is the opposite by taking away the amount of numbers from the original. Examples are 3-2+1 and 9-2=7.

There are many rules to multiplication and division.
Some include:

  • When multiplying a number by zero(0) the answer is always 0.
