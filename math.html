Math

To learn math first you must understand the key elements:

  • Addition "+"
  • Subtraction "-"
  • Multiplication "*"
  • Division "รท"

Adding is when numbers are collectively added to the other for example 1+1=2 or 3+4=7 which can be done by counting on fingers or bigger numbers on the calculator. Subtraction is the opposite by taking away the amount of numbers from the original. Examples are 3-2+1 and 9-2=7.

There are many rules to multiplication and division.
Some include:

  • When multiplying a number by zero(0) the answer is always 0 Example: 25 * 1 = 25.
  • Dividing by zero gives an undefined answer.
  • Any number multiplied by 1 always stays the same. 4 * 1 = 4
  • A whole number multiplied by 10 can just add another 0 at the end. Ex: 2 * 10 = 20

Math Subjects

Some math subjects that are useful to learn are listed below:

  • Decimals and Fractions: 1/2, 2/3, 5/6, 0.3, 0.5.
  • Linear Equations: y=mx+b: an equation of a line where m=slope, b=y-intercept.
  • PEMDAS- Parenthesis, Exponents, Multiplication, Division, Addition, Subtraction.
  • Measurements and data
  • Place value and Rounding
